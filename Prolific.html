About


Living in this fast-moving world and managing multiple activities has become more than just a necessity. Being productive is a sustainable skill that only sharpens more when one adopts it early. Prolific Productivity App is a mobile version of productivity app that targets youngsters to help them develop this skill in their formative years. The age group focused on this app is between 16 to 24 years old. Managing curricular activities, cocurricular activities, preparing for exams and planning your weekly submissions, everything is possible with this app!
